## About the Collection

As a self-taught baker, I know the value of approachable, easy-to-follow recipes the cover a wide array of baking styles. I've used this site to provide a collection of recipes (both original and adapted from other bakers) that are approachable enoguh for a new baker, but still challenging enough to impress your audience.
